Why Animal Welfare Matters

Animals are essential to the balance of our ecosystem and contribute significantly to human well-being—emotionally, environmentally, and socially. From supporting biodiversity to offering companionship, their presence enriches our lives in countless ways.

However, many animals continue to suffer from neglect, abuse,
and lack of proper care. Stray animals struggle for food and shelter, while others face unsafe living conditions or abandonment. Ensuring their safety and dignity is not just an act of kindness—it’s
a responsibility.

Together, let’s give every animal the life they deserve.
